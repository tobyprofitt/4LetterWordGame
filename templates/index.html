How to Play WordWay

In WordWay, your task is to transform a starting word into a target word. You can change one letter at a time, and every intermediate step must be a valid word. You can also rearrange the letters of a word to form another valid word.

Example:

L
I
M
E

↓ (rearrange)

M
I
L
E

↓

M
I
L
K

↓

S
I
L
K

Every move increases your score by one. Try to transform the starting word into the target word in the fewest moves possible!
